Church Ministries

Opportunities to grow, serve, and connect

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry provides a safe and nurturing environment for young people to grow in their faith, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships.

Leader: David Mukasa Saturdays, 2:00 PM

Women's Ministry

The Women's Ministry offers spiritual growth, fellowship, and service opportunities for women of all ages, focusing on Bible study and community outreach.

Leader: Grace Namugga First Tuesday, 4:00 PM

Men's Fellowship

The Men's Fellowship provides opportunities for men to grow spiritually, develop godly character, and serve their families, church, and community.

Leader: James Kato Third Saturday, 7:00 AM

Choir Ministry

Our choir leads the congregation in worship through music, enhancing the worship experience with traditional hymns and contemporary Christian songs.

Leader: Sarah Nalunga Fridays, 4:00 PM

Community Outreach

Our outreach ministry serves the local community through various programs including food distribution, medical camps, and educational support for underprivileged children.

Leader: Peter Mugisha Monthly Activities

Sunday School

Our Sunday School program provides age-appropriate Bible teaching for children, helping them develop a strong foundation of faith from an early age.

Leader: Mary Nakato Sundays, 9:30 AM

How Our Ministries Work

At St. Janani Church, our ministries are designed to help members grow spiritually, use their God-given gifts, and serve others. Each ministry is led by dedicated volunteers who are passionate about their area of service.

Our ministries operate with these core principles:

  • Biblical teaching and discipleship
  • Prayer as the foundation of all activities
  • Inclusive community that welcomes all
  • Service to others in Christ's name
  • Development of leadership skills

How to Get Involved

We encourage all members and visitors to get involved in our ministries based on their interests, skills, and spiritual gifts. Here's how you can join:

  • Attend a ministry meeting or event
  • Speak with ministry leaders after Sunday service
  • Fill out a volunteer form at the welcome desk
  • Contact the church office for more information
  • Participate in our quarterly Ministry Fair

No matter your age, background, or experience, there's a place for you to serve and grow at St. Janani Church.
