WORSHIP SERVICES

Join us in praise, worship, and spiritual nourishment

01

Sunday Morning Service

Our main worship service featuring Holy Communion, inspiring sermons, and vibrant worship music. Starts at 8:00 AM every Sunday.

02

Sunday Evening Service

A more intimate service of prayer and reflection. Held at 5:00 PM every Sunday.

03

Midweek Fellowship

Bible study and prayer meeting every Wednesday at 6:00 PM. Strengthen your faith in community.

04

Choir Practice

Join our choir for weekly rehearsals every Friday at 4:00 PM. All voices welcome!

05

Youth Ministry

Special services and activities for young people every Saturday at 2:00 PM.

06

Sunday School

Bible lessons for children of all ages during Sunday services. Starts at 9:30 AM.

07

Women's Fellowship

Monthly gathering for spiritual growth and community building. First Tuesday of each month.

08

Special Services

Christmas, Easter, Pentecost, and other holy day services throughout the year.
