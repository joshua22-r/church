    Our Mission

    Proclaiming Christ in Uganda since 1877

    The Church of Uganda is a member of the worldwide Anglican Communion. Our mission is to proclaim the Good News of the Kingdom of God, teach all nations to observe Christ's commandments, and provide pastoral care to all God's people.

    Faith Makes Life Better

    At Church of Uganda, we believe that a relationship with Jesus Christ transforms lives. Through worship, fellowship, and service, we help people experience the abundant life that God intends for all His children.
